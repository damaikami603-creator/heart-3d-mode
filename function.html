How the Heart Functions

Your heart's main function is to move blood throughout your body. Blood brings oxygen and nutrients to your cells. It also takes away carbon dioxide and other waste.

Electrical impulses make your heart beat, moving blood through these chambers. Heart valves control the flow of blood so that it moves in the right direction.
