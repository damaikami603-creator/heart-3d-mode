Anatomy of the Heart

The heart is a fist-sized organ that pumps blood throughout your body. It’s your circulatory system’s main organ. Muscle and tissue make up this powerhouse organ. It contains four muscular sections (chambers): left atrium, right atrium, left ventricle, right ventricle.

The heart weighs between 7 and 15 ounces, with a man's heart typically heavier.
