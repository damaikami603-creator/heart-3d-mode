Interesting Scientific Facts

  • Your heart beats over 100,000 times per day.
  • A woman's heart beats faster than a man's by about 8 beats per minute.
  • The heart has its own electrical impulse and can beat independently.
  • There are 60,000 miles of blood vessels in your body.
  • The sound of your heartbeat is the valves opening and closing.
